Featured Resources

Three essential services every Loudoun County resident should know about.

Food Assistance

Loudoun Hunger Relief

Loudoun Hunger Relief operates multiple food pantries across the county, providing nutritious groceries and emergency food boxes to families in need — no income verification required for first-time visits.

  • Leesburg, Sterling & Ashburn locations
  • Mon–Sat, hours vary by location
  • (703) 737-8005
Visit Website
Youth & Education

Loudoun County Boys & Girls Club

Providing after-school programs, summer camps, tutoring, and mentorship for youth ages 6–18 across Loudoun County. Membership is kept affordable to ensure every child has access regardless of family income.

  • Multiple sites across Loudoun County
  • Mon–Fri after school + full days in summer
  • (703) 771-1088
Visit Website
Health Services

Loudoun Free Clinic

Offering free primary care, dental, vision, mental health, and prescription services to uninsured and underinsured Loudoun County residents. Entirely volunteer-run and funded by donations.

  • 607 S. King St., Leesburg, VA 20175
  • Tue & Thu evenings, Sat mornings
  • (703) 779-7699
